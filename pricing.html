frequently asked questions

01
What's included in the basic package?

Our basic package includes a 1-hour photo session, professional editing of 30 selected photos, online gallery access, and digital delivery. Perfect for individual portraits or small projects.

02
How long until I receive my photos?

Standard delivery time is 5-7 business days for basic editing. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee. Wedding and event collections may take 2-3 weeks for full processing.

03
Do you offer video services?

Yes, we provide professional video services including music videos, commercial shoots, and event coverage. Each video package includes pre-production planning, filming, and post-production editing.

04
Can I customize a package?

Absolutely! We understand each project is unique. Contact us to discuss your specific needs and we'll create a custom package that perfectly matches your vision and budget.

05
What about location shoots?

We offer both studio and location photography. For locations within Sofia, travel is included. Additional travel fees may apply for shoots outside the city.

06
Do you provide hair and makeup?

Professional hair and makeup services can be added to any package. We work with experienced stylists who specialize in both natural and glamour looks.
